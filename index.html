FAQs

Common questions, clear answers.

I have not registered with a Pension Fund Administrator. What should I do?
To register with us, simply click here to enroll online, or download our RSA Account Opening Form and submit at any FCMB Pensions office. Find a branch near you.
Can I update my personal details online?
Yes. Log into your client portal, navigate to Bio Data, select Update, and enter your new phone number, email, or address in the "New Information" section. You'll receive an email confirmation once the update is saved.
Can I move my RSA from another Pension Fund Administrator to FCMB Pensions?
Absolutely. RSA holders are permitted to transfer to a different PFA once every year. Start your RSA transfer here — the process is fully online and takes just a few minutes.
What is the Ethical / Non-Interest Fund (Fund VI)?
Fund VI is specifically designed for contributors who prefer Sharia-compliant investing. Your pension contributions are invested exclusively in non-interest (ethical) financial instruments, ensuring your retirement savings align with your values. Learn more about Fund VI.
